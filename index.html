Game Dev Demo Reel 2025

My Work

Rooted

A Global Game Jam 2023 game where you explore Chichén Itzá’s pyramid as a tomb raider, confronting mythical guardians to steal Kukulkan’s enchanted feather headdress.

CATS TD

A tower defense game where cats protect their territory against incoming waves of yarn balls. It features a dynamic difficulty system that scales based on wave progression and player economy, encouraging strategic resource management. It includes a variety of unique cats that can be individually upgraded, unlocking new transformations or special powers to unleash powerful strategies.

Folter Castle

A visual novel/psychological horror game where you play as a kidnapped man, his memory clouded and fragmented by disturbing flashbacks of a large, deformed figure carrying him into a dungeon. Upon waking, you attempt to escape through the eerie halls of Folter Castle, confronting unsettling scenes such as human silhouettes and medieval torture devices. These discoveries lead you to question whether the creature is truly responsible for the horrors within.

Automaton Hunters

Automaton Hunters is an intense VR shooter, to escape the virtual world by defeating waves of enemies mastering a variety of weapons to survive.

AI Sate Machine

A AI state machine goes through several phases. It starts in a “rage” state (indicated by green) before moving into an “alert” phase (indicated by yellow). As the situation deteriorates, the AI enters an aggressive attack mode (indicated by red).

Broken Mind

A puzzle horror/survival game where you awaken in an abandoned school, its corridors echoing with whispers and distant footsteps. Your memory is fractured, with glimpses of a violent incident and a haunting voice urging you to “put the pieces back together.” To escape, you must solve intricate puzzles scattered throughout the school while evading paranormal entities that block your way or hunt you down. Every puzzle solved brings you closer to freedom or to the truth that shattered your mind.

Live Architect

A house visualization and construction tool where users can create architectural models from scratch or import their own 3D assets. The application includes a library of preset shapes for quick prototyping, as well as options to save and export completed projects. Powered by AI, it can generate immersive virtual walkthroughs, allowing realistic presentations of houses, buildings, or scale models for design and planning purposes.

Cutman Engine

CutmanEngine is a graphic motor developed in C++ using Visual Studio and Direct3D for texturing. It manages core components (window, device, swap chain, views). It provides utilities for shaders, texture/model loading, and a scene UI. Its main loop processes messages, updates, and renders real-time graphics.
